About DHI

Design Hub International – practice based in Ukraine, which provides architectural, engineering, building consultancy, design & planning services within CIS region – which offers a broad range of professional outsource services for our international partners.

Our first key advantage is that we have assembled the best professionals on Ukrainian market, which gained their degrees, work experience internationally, and took part in high profile projects in CIS, EEA, Australia and Middle East.

And the second one is that the company is structured as an integrated association of individual professionals and design & engineering teams and operates as a Design and Project Management company which gives us flexibility in delivery projects of different scale and programme conditions keeping operational expenses as low as possible to offer our client best fees.
